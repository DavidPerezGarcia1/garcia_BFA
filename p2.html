Artist Statement

Well growing up as a kid, I was not actually a huge art kid throughout those experiences,
I was normally playing around with my family, playing video games. However, during my 12
years of age and up to now, I realize making art is not only fun but I was actually able to express
myself so much more than me talking to someone somehow. This new change of my idea, I slowly
build up experience and ideas of my own art or other worlds, my own idea of what art should be.
Which is being able to express oneself to the fullest that no simple conversation can out do it.
Starting off with simple drawings of what I like in general,like a video game disc that I played from
my brother or my favorite toy when I was much younger. Unfortunately during that time I wasn't able
to get my own lap or any device to make digital art in general. However, during my teenage years and
now, I have built up my own digital art from a wide variety of different mediums, from photoshop to 3D
models. When talking about digital media art, as soon as I touched the concept of it and the idea of how
to make/display it into digital art, I was hooked on it right away. I instantly wanted to learn more, and
even more than that, to a point it's a lot of struggle and tedious work to handle overall. The more I learn
new methods and subjects like learning 3D modeling for example, it pushes me back from stage one to
learn all together. However, this doesn't stop me, I push myself to learn but also express those models of
what i want it to be, from a model of myself or a character from a show. I learn so much about narration,
creativity in digital media, especially video art and 3D modeling, which I am most passionate about. Digital
media made me think heavily about why, what is the purpose of this art work i am making, for example, I
can me cool looking model or lots of brush function in p5.js, however, in my opinion, it doesn't mean
anything if there is no purpose behind it form first place, must have story in way to see and act. In my
opinion, the fact that I am able to achieve the exact outcome I wanted from any digital medium, is the best
part of making out with, learning how to work around issues that I am unable to fix, and really push myself
as an artist of being forced into issues that I normally dont face. This is why i want to pursue more into
digital medium as a whole, by pushing myself, even though I admit have weak point in parts of my art like
coding for example, but its those realization of knowing my flaws that only improve myself more as digital
artist or otherwise how can i stand my own meaning as artist.
